About the App

BLS Event Tracker provides community-driven updates on:

  • Road conditions (plowed or blocked)
  • Power outages

Users can submit reports, confirm updates, or flag incorrect information to help keep the community informed.

Privacy & Safety

  • No personal information is displayed publicly
  • Users can report inappropriate or incorrect content
  • Accounts can be deleted at any time within the app

For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
